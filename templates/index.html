Problems

Sea otter need to be in cold spaces!

sea otters and other animals are dying because of global warming. Sea otters need to live in cold places but since it's getting warmer they are having a hard time

125,000 sea otters only living

Oil Spills

Oil spills are a problem sea otters are facing because if oil is all around the ocean then, the oil probably touch the sea otter’s fur. Since sea otters groom themselves a lot, they might get sick or poisoned.

Harmful Waste

Waste from land such as chemicals or plastic can hurt sea otters. For example if poisonous chemicals or plastic goes on the food or animals the sea otters eat the sea otters will probably die.
